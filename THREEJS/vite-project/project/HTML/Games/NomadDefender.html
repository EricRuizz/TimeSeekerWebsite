CONSOLE DIALOGUE

Fully designed and implemented, great in terms of atmosphere and immersion
The Console Dialogue System is fairly simple, yet the Text Decoder is the greatest piece of technology I programmed in this project. The Text Decoder is a script that allows any text to be animated, used in every text of the game, from the Main Menu, the Cards, Difficulty Selector to the Console Dialogue
various configurations using ScriptableObjects with a wide variety of options to ensure the maximum amount of control over the end result.
